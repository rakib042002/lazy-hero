MINT PRICE?

THE Mint price is set at 1 sol per lazy hero.

IS THERE A WHITELIST?

Yes! Absolutely.
500 spots are available for whitelisting. Hop on our discord and refer 5 friends to get your spot. We also require you to be an active community member!
You will be guaranteed to mint a Lazy Hero before the public minting starts. Limited at 1 per wallet for pre-sale.

what is the LAZY HEROES VAULT?

Heroes are selfless! 20% of the royalties are going to the vault.
It's a community fund that will be allocated to the Lazy Heroes DAO and community incentives.

When & where do they DROP?

We know you guys are very excited! The Official launch date is October 21st.
Minting will be live on the website at 9PM UTC. On the launch day, we're aiming for MagicEden, DigitalEyes, Solanart and AlphaArtMarket.
